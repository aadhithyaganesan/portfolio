About Me

Hey there! I'm Aadhithya Ganesan, a UX Designer and multidisciplinary artist based in Toronto.

I grew up with an interest in the arts, exploring my ideas by drawing, painting, sculpting, writing, making stop-motion films and editing videos. During my undergraduate Computer Science program at Toronto Metropolitan University, I discovered UX Design as the next step in my artistic pursuit, with the ultimate goal of making systems user friendly.

I had the opportunity to kickoff my career at the Ontario Ministry of Transportation, where I familiarized myself to the SDLC (System Development Lifecycle) and trained in key facets of UX Design such as design thinking, user research, interviews, synthesis, user-centric design, prototyping, user testing, and design sprints. As a UX Designer, I am driven to create experiences that are intuitive, immersive and impactful to the user.

In my free time, I'm either watching movies or talking about them. I also love chilling with my cat, Lotus.
